The Department of Computational and Data Sciences at IISER Kolkata, established in 2019, offers education and research opportunities in various cutting-edge fields, including Information Retrieval (IR), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML), and Data Mining. With esteemed faculty specializing in these areas, the department fosters innovation and excellence. It provides Ph.D. and integrated Ph.D. programs, creating a vibrant learning environment for students. The department's collaborative approach and impactful research output establish it as a center of excellence in science and technology.
